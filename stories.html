Between Glances

She sees him every day, and each time, her heart races a little faster, hoping for something more than the casual, fleeting glances they exchange. He's charming, quiet, and always so close-within arm's reach, yet always just out of her grasp. Their interactions are brief, often accompanied by a charged silence that neither dares to break. There's an unspoken tension between them, a magnetic pull that neither seems able to resist, but something always keeps them from stepping past that delicate line between familiarity and desire. Whether it's the weight of their unacknowledged feelings or the fear of disrupting the fragile balance they've created, they remain suspended in a world of what-ifs. As their glances linger longer and the silence stretches between them, the question remains: will they find the courage to close the distance, or will they continue dancing around their feelings, too afraid to leap into the unknown?

The Boy Next Door

Sarah never thought much of the boy who lived a few houses down-until college brought them back together. After a night of chatting and a movie at her place, something changed. Her dog, usually affectionate, refused to sit next to him, and Sarah couldn't shake the unsettling feeling that followed. She quickly pushed him out the door, but now, even though she hasn't seen him since, the unease lingers. What is the boy next door hiding?

Chilled Encounters

As a transfer sophomore, Emily isn't expecting much from her first class of the semester-until a cocky freshman, Ryan, walks in. At first, she's charmed by his looks, but his arrogant attitude and sharp comments quickly turn her off. Although she vents to her friend about him, Emily's introverted nature keeps her from confronting Ryan directly. But when ski season arrives, everything changes. Emily discovers she shares mutual friends with Ryan and spots him skiing outside her dorm window. Taking a chance, she steps outside to say hi, and for the first time, they talk. The tension between them begins to thaw, and what started as an infuriating encounter might just lead to something unexpected.

Three's Temptation

Caught in a web of desire, Violet, Leo, and Felix find themselves navigating the uncharted waters of a passionate and complicated love triangle. What began as a bond forged by shared moments and growing attraction soon spirals into a tumultuous emotional storm. Violet, the quiet and reserved heart of their trio, struggles with the intensity of her feelings for both Leo and Felix, each representing a different side of the love she craves-Leo, the strong, silent protector who offers unwavering stability, and Felix, the charismatic, carefree spirit who brings out her wild side. As their connections deepen, jealousy, longing, and confusion begin to cloud their once-clear paths, forcing them to question their desires, their choices, and the boundaries of love itself. The three must confront their own fears of vulnerability, trust, and the consequences of their actions as they teeter on the brink of losing what they share-whether to their own emotional turbulence or the harsh realities of what it means to love more than one person at once. Each choice they make carries a weight that could either bind them closer together or unravel everything they've come to mean to one another. In this story of love, passion, and self-discovery, Violet, Leo, and Felix must decide whether their hearts can remain intertwined or if they are destined to drift apart forever, lost in the tangled web of their emotions.

Triple Passion Unleashed

Life is turned upside down when two men from different walks of her life converge, forcing her to confront the desires and emotions she's been trying to suppress. Charlie, the brooding and intense individual she's been avoiding for a year, has reappeared, his piercing gaze making her skin crawl. On the other hand, there's Nick, the quiet and dependable guy who has a way of making her feel at ease, his presence a comforting refuge from the turmoil that Charlie stirs up. As fate would have it, the three of them find themselves in the same room, the air thick with tension. With Charlie's dark passion and Nick's gentle charm pulling her in opposite directions, Jane is faced with a daunting choice: will she surrender to the thrill of the unknown with Charlie, seek the safe haven Nick provides, or open herself up to both? As the sparks of desire, jealousy, and temptation ignite, Jane must decide whether to flee or face the consequences of her heart's deepest desires.

Four Burning Desires

Ava's quiet life at college in Burlington, Vermont, is about to be upended by three guys who each want something from her-and they're not afraid to go after it. When she invites Jake, Matt, and Carter to join her at her family's lake house for a weekend getaway, she expects nothing more than a relaxing break. But the tension between the four of them is palpable, and Ava can feel it in every moment. Jake, with his cocky charm, always knows how to make her heart race. Matt, with his playful flirtations, makes her feel like she's the only one in the room. And Carter, quiet and intense, has a way of looking at her that makes her question everything she thought she knew about herself. As the weekend heats up, so do the emotions-and the lines between friendship and something more become dangerously blurred. With the pressure of their unspoken desires growing stronger, Ava is forced to confront the undeniable chemistry between her and the guys, knowing that this weekend could change everything. But who will she choose when the sparks fly, and who will be left behind in the burn?

Beneath the Jolly Roger

Time is a tide, and she's about to be swept away. Eleanor "Ellie" Barnes is a brilliant but restless history student, obsessed with the myths and mysteries of the Golden Age of Piracy. When she stumbles upon a centuries-old artifact buried in a coastal shipwreck site, she doesn't expect it to drag her straight into the past-1702, the Caribbean, and the brutal world of cutthroats and corsairs. Alone and out of place, Ellie is forced aboard a pirate ship and into the orbit of a charismatic young sailor named Edward Teach-not yet the feared Blackbeard, but well on his way. As Ellie searches for a way home, she and Teach strike an uneasy alliance. He needs her strange knowledge, and she needs his help to survive-and find the artifact that can send her back. But time doesn't give up its secrets easily. Betrayal, mutiny, and war threaten to tear them apart. When Ellie finally returns to the present, the world she once knew feels foreign and flat. Haunted by what she left behind-and who-she makes a dangerous decision: to return to the past, where freedom sails under the black flag, and a pirate waits with the storm in his eyes.

He Knows Where You Sleep

Sophie Lane is used to flying under the radar. Smart, quiet, and reserved, she blends into the background at her college-until she gathers the courage to ask out the one guy who seems completely out of her league. Ethan Vale is everything she's not: effortlessly confident, a little reckless, and the center of attention without even trying. When he says yes, Sophie is shocked-and immediately backs out, overwhelmed by doubt. She tries to move on, brushing it off as a moment of boldness gone wrong. But Ethan doesn't forget. And he doesn't stop watching. At first, Sophie thinks she's imagining it. The lingering glances across campus. The way his friends always seem to know where she is. The familiar faces near her dorm, even though she's rarely there-always buried in assignments, studying in the library, or spending late nights in the lab. The only place she's never seen him? The forensic lab. Her one safe haven. For now. Then a friend casually mentions Ethan's name-along with the word "psychopath" and whispers of incidents no one dares speak about. Assault. Harassment. Warnings that were never taken seriously. Suddenly, everything starts to click into place. She regrets ever speaking to him. But it might already be too late. Because Ethan Vale isn't just watching anymore. He's waiting.
